  • 1997
    •  Alexandre le Bienheureux Alexandre, a good-natured and nonchalant man, is a farmer on a French farm in Beauce. However, his daily life is directed by "the Great", his ambitious but tyrannical wife, who exhausts him by imposing an excessive list of tasks on him every day. Having suddenly become a widower, he experiences great relief and feels freed from his work: he decides to take the rest he deems deserved, in order to take the time to savor life.
